About Me

Born and raised in Lincoln, Nebraska, I find myself dreaming of leaving the midwest the first chance I get. I didn't get very far when it came time to go attend school at the university level, but I did take advantage of many traveling opportunities leading up to graduation this spring.

Due to the pandemic of 2020, I have taken a step back from traveling and instead have been working at CVS pharmacy where I conduct testing for the COVID-19 virus. I am also spending many hours practicing new programming languages while also playing around with what I already know!
